Safety Information

Important guidelines for safe and enjoyable fragrance use

Product Safety & Ingredients

All Low Tide Fragrances are formulated with carefully selected ingredients that meet international safety standards. Our fragrances are alcohol-based and contain natural and synthetic essences.

Key Safety Features

  • Dermatologically tested and approved for safe skin contact
  • All ingredients meet IFRA (International Fragrance Association) guidelines
  • Free from harmful heavy metals and toxic substances
  • Hypoallergenic formulations
  • Cruelty-free and not tested on animals

⚠️ Important Precautions

Do not apply to broken or irritated skin, eyes, or mucous membranes. Avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water and seek medical advice if irritation persists.

Proper Usage Guidelines

How to Use:

  • Apply to pulse points: wrists, neck, behind ears, and inside elbows
  • Spray from 6-8 inches away from the skin for even distribution
  • Use 2-4 sprays depending on preference
  • Do not rub wrists together
  • For longer lasting fragrance, apply to moisturized skin

Storage Recommendations:

  • Store in a cool, dark, and dry place (60-70°F ideal)
  • Keep away from direct sunlight and extreme heat
  • Store away from high humidity environments
  • Keep the cap tightly closed to prevent evaporation
  • Avoid storing in bathrooms

📋 First Aid Information

If ingested: Contact Poison Control at 1-800-222-1222 (US) immediately.

If eye contact occurs: Rinse eyes gently with lukewarm water for at least 15 minutes.

If skin irritation develops: Wash affected area with mild soap and water. If irritation persists after 24 hours, consult a dermatologist.
