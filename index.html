Safety Information

Usage Guidelines

  • Apply to pulse points: wrists, behind ears, and inner elbows
  • Use sparingly - a small amount goes a long way
  • Do not spray directly on fabric unless tested first
  • Avoid contact with eyes
  • For sensitive skin, perform a patch test before use

Storage Recommendations

  • Store in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep away from extreme heat or cold
  • Avoid humid environments like bathrooms
  • Keep bottle tightly sealed when not in use
  • Store away from children and pets

First Aid

  • Eye Contact: Rinse immediately with plenty of water for at least 15 minutes. Seek medical attention if irritation persists.
  • Skin Irritation: Wash affected area thoroughly with mild soap and water. Consult a dermatologist if necessary.
  • Ingestion: Do not ingest. If swallowed, seek medical attention immediately.
